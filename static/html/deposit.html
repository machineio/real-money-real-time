How Can I Deposit?

When you are logged in your account, click the “Deposit” tab that you'll see at the top of your screen. It will give you options (Visa, MasterCard, etc) to choose from. Fill out your personal details as requested. Confirmation that your deposit was successful, will be send to your email for confirmation, to keep for your records. The new balance will be reflected in your account immediately. At that point you will be able to start trading.

Our payment methods

We have a number of different payment options for our traders including all major credit cards, (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover), or a bank wire.

Deposit Via Credit Card

Easiest and quickest method is the credit card. All funding done with a credit or debit card will be automatically credited to your account. Most debit/ATM cards are accepted as long as they have a VISA or MasterCard logo. American Express and Discover payments, will require you to contact customer service, by email (info@binarymachine.io) or telephone. (1­855­861­4975).

Bank Wire deposit

Please contact customer service when you wish to do a bank wire, for we will need certain informationbefore sending you our wire instructions. When you received them, please print out the wire details and bring them to your bank. Once your bank process your wire, please be sure to take and save a copy of the wire receipt your bank gives you. Please forward this receipt directly to our customer support so that it can be processed as fast as possible.

Important, bank wires can take up to 3 business days to appear in your bank account and banking fees may be charged. Please also note that any wires sent in a currency other than the United States dollar will be charged a conversion fee by our bank.

Withdrawals

In order to ensure the fastest and most efficient withdrawal of your money, please refer to our Terms and Conditions and don’t hesitate to contact us for more information or instructions about our withdrawal procedure.
