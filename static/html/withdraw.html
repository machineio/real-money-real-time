How Can I Withdraw?

Online withdrawals are fast and secure. If you are in need of assistance during the process, please contact us via phone, or email to: info@binarymachine.io

How can I make a request to withdraw funds from my account?

Log-in to your Deep Machine account. Click Budget on the top of the page, and click on Withdraw on the dropdown menu. Enter the amount of your withdrawal and press apply. You will be notified via email once the processing of your withdrawal is complete, or if further information is required.

What documents are required for a first time withdrawal?

Documents required for account verification:

Clear copies of Photo Identification and Credit Card used for your transaction

  • Photo ID – Must be a government issued ID (ex.: Driver’s License, Passport or ID Card)
  • Credit Card – Front and back copies are both necessary (feel free to cover the first 12-digits, as long as the last 4 digits and name are visible on the card).

Credit Card Authorization Form – Will be sent as a Docusign Form to be completed and signed.

Withdrawal requests submitted without aforementioned documentation has been submitted will be automatically cancelled.

How long does it take to process a withdrawal?

Once a withdrawal requested is submitted, we may take up to 3 business days to process the request. Once your request is approved, it may take an additional 5 to 7 business days for the funds to be credited to your account.

Are there any withdrawal fees?

There is no fee relating to withdrawals by Credit Card, but any withdrawal by wire transfer will be charged with the amount of $25.

Withdrawals under the amount of $100 will not be processed. Please note that withdrawals will go back through the same method of deposit you used.

Why is my withdrawal request being cancelled?

If your withdrawal request has been cancelled it can be one of two things; you haven’t submitted the proper documentation for verification process or you have accepted a bonus and haven’t completed the terms of the bonus. All bonuses given to your account have a 30 time rollover requirement.

(For further explanation, please refer to point 12 on Terms and Conditions page)

12: To withdraw your funds, you will be asked to perform a trading volume of at least 30 times for each $1 bonus. Any funds in your account, including but not limited to your initial deposit, can be withdrawn only when the foregoing stipulation has been fully respected and fulfilled. Any attempt to withdrawal funds from an account made before completing the terms of the bonus will be immediately canceled and the request will be removed from the account.

Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

Yes, you can cancel your withdrawal request at any time by contacting us via telephone, live chat or via email: info@binarymachine.io
